Welcome! I'm Ken, and this is where I'll be sharing what I learn, what I think about, and the occasional "30 days of learning something new" experiment.

## What to expect

I write for curious, non-technical people — the kind of person who wants to understand *why* something matters, not just *how* it works. Topics will range across:

- **Finance & VC** — What I've learned from evaluating startups and building financial models
- **Systems thinking** — How to design processes that actually work
- **AI & tools** — Practical applications that make a real difference
- **Learning experiments** — Documenting what happens when you commit to learning something for 30 days straight

## Why write?

Mostly because writing forces clarity. If I can't explain something simply, I probably don't understand it well enough. And if what I write happens to be useful to someone else — even better.
